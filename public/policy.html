Privacy Policy for VDX Pixel Tracker

Effective Date: February 16, 2025

Last Updated: February 16, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The VDX Pixel Tracker extension does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). However, it processes network request data to detect and validate pixel tracking in VDX ad units.

The types of data processed include:

  • URLs of network requests related to VDX ad units
  • Status of pixel firing (success/failure)
  • Timestamps of network requests

2. How We Use the Data

The data processed by the extension is used solely for:

  • Tracking and validating pixel firing in VDX ad units
  • Helping advertisers and developers ensure accurate ad tracking
  • Debugging and troubleshooting tracking-related issues

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Uses chrome.storage to store user preferences and logs locally.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • No cookies or analytics tracking are used.

4. Permissions Justification

The extension requires certain Chrome permissions:

  • webRequest: To monitor network requests for pixel validation.
  • activeTab & tabs: To track pixel activity on VDX ad unit pages.
  • storage: To save user preferences and debugging logs.
  • scripting & declarativeNetRequest: To inject scripts for tracking.

These permissions are used only for the stated purpose and do not track user activity beyond VDX ad units.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension does not integrate with third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

6. Your Rights

  • You can disable or remove the extension at any time from Chrome settings.
  • You can clear stored data by resetting the extension settings.
  • We do not store any user data beyond temporary logs for tracking validation.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in the Last Updated date.
